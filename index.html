Welcome to PushMachine

PushMachine allows you to seamlessly push blog posts, videos, photos and tweets to all of your social feeds.







How Does It Work?

So, there are 4 tabs in PushMachine:

The first one allows you to write blog posts in the markdown form, then publish them to all of your blog services with a tick system so you can choose which articles go where.

The next tab lets you upload videos to all of your video services e.g. YouTube and Vimeo, and it allows you to choose which ones you like with a tick system, like the previous tab. You can also easily set a thumbnail, title, description and tags.

The third tab can post short text to your social media accounts that are like that such as Twitter and Facebook, and there is a character length in place so you don't accidentally write too much.

The last tab allows you to post images, videos and stories to Instagram.

And the best part? It's completely free, with no branding on posts, no advertisements and no hidden charges.
